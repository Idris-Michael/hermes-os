## Users
Our users are advanced AI developers, sovereign engineers, and content directors who operate autonomous multi-agent systems, local pipelines (TTS, UGC, Crons), and memory networks. They expect a "Command Center" interface that feels elite, atmospheric, responsive, and visually packed with real-time operations.

## Brand & Aesthetic
- **Visual Style:** High-end premium cyberpunk command center. Glassmorphism, deep translucent dark overlays, neon grid systems, floating particle fields, and glowing suit borders.
- **Accents:** High-contrast desaturated primary colors. Emerald green (`#00ffcc` or `#10b981`) for connections and online statuses, gold (`#D4A017` or `#f59e0b`) for primary controls and strategic badges, and vivid pink/violet (`#ff00cc` or `#7c3aed`) for growth and creative profiles.
- **Materiality:** 1px inner translucent borders (`border-white/10`), inner light refractions (`shadow-[inset_0_1px_0_rgba(255,255,255,0.1)]`), deep blurred overlays (`backdrop-blur-md bg-black/40`), and magnetic interaction loops.
- **Tone:** Professional, precise, high-agency, command-oriented. Zero fluff.

## Strategic Principles
1. **Never Boxes for Boxes:** Visual density should be high but cards are used intentionally. Grid spacing, fine 1px lines, and negative space should structure the data.
2. **Interactive States Mandatory:** Tactile feedback on click (`scale-[0.98]`), animated transitions on state change, and glowing interactive hover states.
3. **No Slop:** Completely ban generic names (e.g. John Doe), generic icons, and linear easing. Always use spring animations and authentic high-fidelity metadata.
